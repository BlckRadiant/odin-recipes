Delicious Shrimp Alfredo

Creamy Shrimp Alfredo made with Love

This easy shrimp Alfredo pasta recipe is the perfect fast weeknight
brdinner that's tasty enough for company! Tender shrimp and fettuccine
pasta are smothered in an amazing creamy garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Shrimp (20-30 Count)
  • Butter (unsalted)
  • Cream cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add the butter, cream cheese, heavy cream, chicken broth,
    and garlic to a skillet over medium heat. Cook it for 5 minutes;
  2. Stir in the parmesan cheese and let it bubble for about a minute;
  3. Add the shrimp and cook for 5-6 minutes;
  4. Season to taste and toss with the pasta.
