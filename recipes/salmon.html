Pan Seared Salmon

Garlic Butter Seared Salmon

Pan Seared Salmon are super crispy on the outside, while soft, tender
and flakey on the inside, finished with a silky, flavourful buttery sauce.

Ingredients

  • Skinless salmon fillets
  • Lemon jice
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Dry room temperature salmon filets with paper towel.
  2. Season all over with salt and pepper.
  3. Squeeze lemon juice over each fillet.
  4. Heat oil in a large non-stick pan or skillet over medium-high heat until hot.
  5. Sear salmon flesh side down, pressing them lightly so the entire surface
    of the flesh comes into contact with the pan, until crispy and golden.
  6. Flip and sear the other side until the outside is crispy.
  7. Add in the butter, garlic, parsley and lemon juice, stirring the melting butter
    around each fillet. (The butter will begin to brown slightly.)
